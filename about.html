ABOUT

Introduction

• Chitaitai Chemicals (Pvt) Ltd is a privately owned business that was incorporated under the laws of Zimbabwe on the 6th of December 2010.

• The Company started by offering floor polish to residential areas around Zimbabwe.

• Currently Chitaitai Chemicals’ products are being supplied in most parts of the country.

Mission Statement

To provide efficient, unmatched professional services countrywide whilst maintaining strong relationships with clients based on transparency, diligence, integrity and thorough knowledge of customer needs.

Vision

 to lead the industry in creating value for our customers and sales channels

 to provide specialist services, in all residential and commercial industries on small and large scales.

 to refocus on the value we offer our consumers and increase operational excellence.

 Develop , Formulates and Implements the organization’s market growth strategies

Objectives & Values

In our endeavor to provide an efficient and reliable customer service to all industry, our operations are guided by the following principles and values;

• Integrity

• Excellence

• Quality

• Professionalism

• Teamwork

• Achievement of total customer satisfaction

• Efficient deliveries

• Rational Costs
